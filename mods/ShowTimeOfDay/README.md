Shows in-game time besides map name, when alarm clock is equipped.
---
Inspired by [Show Time of Day When Looking At Placed Alarm Clock](https://modworkshop.net/mod/54878) by [promitheas](https://modworkshop.net/user/promitheas).

---
Note: When equipping/unequipping alarm clock, it would take ~1 second to properly update the label.
This is an intentional trade off to save some performance.
The reason is, I am updating the label every 300th physics tick instead of every frame.
